Whether discussing cities, companies, knowledge systems or AI agents, decline often comes from broken feedback loops rather than isolated failures.

Renewal is achieved by restoring critical loops rather than optimizing metrics.

The key question becomes:

Where is the bottleneck that breaks the cycle?

Examples:

City:
Population → Jobs → Industry → Population

Knowledge:
Narrative → Concept → Insight → Policy → Action → Narrative

Agent:
Context → Policy → Action → Feedback → Learning

The essence of revitalization is rebuilding self-reinforcing loops.
